FAQ

1. How do I book an appointment?

- visiting our website, or using our online booking system.

2. What should I do if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

We request a minimum of 24-48 hoursnotice for cancellations or rescheduling.

3. What if I'm running late for my appointment?

If you're running late, please let us know. We may need to adjust your treatment time to accommodate our schedule.

4. Are the products you use safe for sensitive skin or allergies?

We use high-quality, professional-grade products.

5.Do you offer walk-ins?

We recommend making an appointment to ensure availability. However, we do accept walk-ins based on availability

6. What types of nail services do you offer?

We offer a variety of services, including manicures, pedicures, gel nails, acrylic nails, nail art, and more.

7. Do you provide gift certificates?

Yes! We offer gift certificates for all of our services, which can be purchased in-store or online.

8. How can I ensure my manicure or pedicure lasts longer?

To extend the life of your nails, avoid exposing them to harsh chemicals, and moisturize regularly.
