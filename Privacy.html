Privacy Policy

At Milina nail Salon, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect data such as your name, contact details, and appointment history to provide and improve our services. We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties, except with trusted service providers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you:
- Make an appointment.
- Provide your contact details.
- Fill out forms or surveys.
- Make a purchase or transaction.
- Use our website or social media pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:.
- To provide and manage the services you request .
- To send appointment reminders and promotional offers.
- To process payments for services rendered.
- To improve our services and customer experience.
- To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as:
- Payment processors
- Appointment scheduling platforms
- Marketing and communication tools.
These third parties are obligated to protect your information and only use it as necessary to provide services on our behalf.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard measures to protect it. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access and review the personal information we hold about you
- Request that we correct any inaccuracies in your information
- Request that we delete your information, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it
- To exercise these rights, please contact us at email address .

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

If you visit our website, we may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These technologies collect information about how you interact with our website, which helps us improve its functionality and tailor our marketing efforts. You can adjust your browser settings to reject cookies, but this may affect your experience on our site.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
